# ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china.png)零、写在前面<br>![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk.png)0. Preface

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)随着智能手机技术的不断进步及Windows系统对原自带扫雷游戏的不断弱化，手机中的扫雷APP在大约十年前开始走进人们的视野，且其玩家群体迅速扩大，至今各手机扫雷APP用户数量已远超电脑扫雷。在Windows系统基本完全放弃原扫雷游戏的今天，手机扫雷APP奇迹般地为这款已有近30年历史的游戏注入了新的活力。时至今日，手机扫雷APP中的玩家成绩已有了大幅度的提升，目前已知的sub40玩家3人，sub50玩家至少已有十余人。但是，目前仍尚未有一篇系统性的手机扫雷教程面世，**多数玩家仅局限于扫雷基本规则和简单的优化操作，而未能充分地在触屏设备上扬长避短**。这在一定程度上阻碍了玩家水平的继续提高。笔者基于自身的手机扫雷心得，试图总结手机扫雷游戏中的进阶提速方法，供各位参考并待后来者完善。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)By the ever-enhancing of smart phone technology and ever-weakening role of minesweeper in Windows systems, mobile minesweeper apps have been playing increasingly important roles in the last 10 years, leading to a rapidly growing community of mobile minesweepers. At present, there are far more mobile minesweeper players than PC minesweeper players. Mobile minesweeper apps miraculously rescued this 30-year-old game which has been abandoned by Windows. Nowadays, mobile minesweeper players have achieved far better scores than before with 3 known sub40 players and at least 10 sub50 players. However, no systematic mobile minesweeper tutorial has been published yet. **Most players only know basic rules and elementary tricks, and are not able to exploit advantages and avoid disadvantages brought by touchscreen devices.** This in some way prevents them from improving. I have summarized several advanced skills based on my mobile sweeping experience. I share them here both for your reference and for possible further refinement.

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)掌握本篇中进阶方法的**前提是能够熟练、快速地判雷**，主要的受众也许是手机高级50-70秒的玩家。其余玩家可适当参考，不必求全部看懂。另外，由于笔者本人水平限制，**本篇不涉及**游戏过程中（此处“过程中”不包括“多指开局”）的**双手操作、长按操作及切换模式的操作**。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)To master the advanced skills in this article, you have to be equipped with **continuous, fast and accurate pattern recognition.** So the primary audience of this artical might be mobile players who have 50s-70s on expert. For worse players, understanding only part of this article is better than nothing. In addition, tricks like **double-hand, long-hold and mode-switching are not involved** due to my lack of these skills.

# ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china.png)一、手机扫雷和电脑扫雷在提速方面的**优劣比较**<br>![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk.png)1. The difference between mobile minesweeper and PC minesweeper

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)众所周知，在判雷基本无障碍的前提下，竞速扫雷是一款操作游戏。下面就简单分析一下手机扫雷和电脑扫雷在操作方面的优劣势。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)It's well-known that if you have precise pattern knowledge, speed minesweeper is an operation game. I'd simply compare the goods and bads between APPs operation and PC operation as following.

## ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china.png)（一）手机扫雷操作的**优势**<br>![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk.png)(1) The operation advantages of APPs than PC

1. ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)手机扫雷只需“用手指点击眼睛看到的地方”，电脑扫雷则需“用眼睛和手控制鼠标点击眼睛看到的地方”，前者的神经反射过程比后者简单得多。<br>
   ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)You only need to Use Your Finger to Tap Where You See on smart phone, but you need to Use Your Eyes and Hands to Control Your Mouse to Click Where You See, APPs sweeping works through more simpler reflexes than PC.

2. ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)由于上一条所述的原因，手机扫雷操作过程中可以**灵活地长距离移动、改变点击方向**，而用鼠标进行类似操作则会失误率高、消耗过多体力。<br>
   ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)Because of the reason above, in the process of APPs sweeping, you can **Flexibly Move Long-Range and Turn Clicking Direction,** but it increases the risks of misclicking and wasting too much energy doing similar operations on your mouse.

3. ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)手机扫雷可以方便地进行双指/多指操作，对于不会使用双指/多指操作的玩家来说，仅使用“多指开局”便可获得极大的增益。而电脑扫雷则无法同时点击盘面中的两处区域。<br>
   ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)It's very convenience to use your multiple fingers on smart phone APPs, to apply Multi-Finger Start will benefit a lot, even for the players who aren't familiar with multiple fingers skill. But you can't do things like this on PC.

4. ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)手机扫雷的全标模式下，无需进行左右键切换即可完成“标雷”和“双击”操作，这使得食指中指配合不协调的玩家也能够用手机全标扫出相当好的成绩。<br>
   ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)In FL mode of APPs, you don't need to switch Left Button and Right Button to switch Flagging and Chording, which allows players whose index finger and middle finger being inharmonic to achieve great score.

## ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china.png)（二）手机扫雷操作的**劣势**<br>![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk.png)(2) The operation disadvantages of APPs than PC

1. ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)手机扫雷过程中，手和手指会对**视线**造成极大的**遮挡**，相比之下电脑中光标的遮挡则基本可忽略。<br>
   ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)In the process of APPs sweeping, your hands and fingers are greatly **Blocking Your Eyesight**, when your mouse cursor rarely making a difference.

2. ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)手机扫雷格子调大则需要**滑屏**（横向高级理论上至少需滑3\~4次），对连续操作和判雷均造成干扰；若为了避免滑屏将格子调小，则更容易点不准。电脑扫雷则完全不存在该问题。<br>
   ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)Big cells bring **Dragging on APPs** (theoretically at least 3\~4 times needed at landscape expert level), which interrupts consecutive operations and pattern recognition. If you apply small cells to avoid dragging operation, then you would suffer misclicking. This problem doesn’t play on PC.

3. ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)电脑扫雷点击鼠标时，弹起按键可以利用微动回弹的力量；而点击手机屏幕时只能依靠手的力量按下、抬起、按下、再抬起……因此手机（单指的）极限点击速度应慢于电脑。<br>
   ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)Mouse budge springback will help you releasing up buttons on PC, but the only force you can use pressing down and up on APPs is from your hands, so the theoretical highest click rate of a single finger on APPs should be lower than on PC.

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)总的来说，在不考虑游戏过程中的双手操作的前提下，笔者认为在操作方式上，手机与电脑虽各有优劣，但手机扫雷的劣势更大，取得好成绩也更难。但根据个人及其他玩家的经验，在单指、有滑屏、无长按、不切换模式的前提下，NF达到42s左右、全标达到48s左右并不是特别难。下面就谈一谈实战操作中如何**扬长避短**、提升成绩。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)In summary, not considering Bimanual Operation, both APPs and PC have their goods and bads, but APPs have more disadvantages than PC, it's also harder to get great score on APPs than PC. But according to the experience of mine and other players, not considering Bimanual Technique, Long-Tap, Mode Switching, it's not very difficult to achieve 42s NF and 48s FL at expert level. I'd share how to **Maximum Goods and Avoid Bads** and then reach higher level below.

# ![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china.png)二、手机扫雷操作的总体原则<br>![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk.png)2. The general principle of APPs minesweeper operations

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)考虑到`Time=bbbv/(cls*IOE)`（或`Time=bbbv/(Ces*ThrP)`），在判雷基本无障碍的前提下，影响扫雷成绩的主要因素应是**操作效率**（即IOE或ThrP）和**局中停顿**（停顿事实上直接导致了cls或Ces降低）。

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)Considering `Time=bbbv/(cls×IOE)`（Or `Time=bbbv/(Ces×ThrP)`）, if you know patterns well enough, the main factor of minesweeper score should be **Efficiency** (i.e. IOE or ThrP) and **Pause in the Game** (which actually brings down the cls or Ces).

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)前者在NF模式下基本只受到**破空**（即点开op）操作的影响，在全标模式下则与**标雷**及**双击位置**有关。破空目前已有较为成熟的教程（见张少武[《一支穿云箭——“破空”简要教程》](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/28703132)）；标雷双击尽管没有针对全标的教程，但目标与常规FL是一致的——标尽可能少的雷、用尽可能少的点击、开尽可能多的格子，判雷过关的前提下多加练习并学习高玩录像（推荐鞠泽恩、朱耀宇的电脑扫雷录像，林锦帆的手机扫雷录像；**全标尤其推荐胡恩彬的电脑扫雷录像**，操作套路性非常强）即可提高（据悉朱耀宇计划推出FL教程）。并且，上述内容在电脑端和手机端并无较大区别。综合以上原因，本篇基本不对其作详细讲解。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)In NF mode, the former factor is only influenced by **Opening Breaking**. In FL mode, the former is about the **Position of Flagging and Chording**. About Opening Breaking, mature tutorial has been published (see [An Arrow Break Through The Mist——A Condensed Guide of Opening Breaking](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/28703132) by Shao-Wu Zhang). About Flagging and Chording, although mature tutorial hasn't been published, both APPs and PC minesweeper share the same target (flagging as less mines as possible, clicking as less as possible, opening as many cells as possible), after you master patterns, practise and practise, and learn top-players' replays (I prefer Ze-En Ju and Yao-Yu Zhu's PC replays and Jin-Fan Lin's APPs replays. **If you are all flagging style, En-Bin Hu's PC replays are the most recommended,** who has a extreme modeling style), and then you will make a further step (I heard a FL tutorial is planing by Yao-Yu Zhu). There're no significant difference in these area between PC and APPs, so I rarely mention this part here.

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)后者则可以详细分为若干类。在电脑扫雷中主要表现为**反应时间**（翻开某格后利用该格信息进行下一步判断，一般会造成0.3\~0.5s停顿）和长距离移动（一方面受到移动速度的限制，另一方面长距离移动后往往需要重新定位鼠标）。而手机扫雷（熟练后）可基本忽略长距离移动的影响，但**滑屏操作**（滑屏时基本无法点击，也切断了判雷的连续性）则会带来更多的停顿。手机扫雷操作的总体原则便是：**充分利用手机扫雷的优势、摆脱其劣势，尽量减少局中停顿**。下面具体对其进行阐述。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)It's possible to categorize the latter factor in detail. **Reaction Duration** (to decide the next step using the information of the cell just opened, generally cause 0.3\~0.5s pause) and Long-Range Movement (restricted by movespeed, also a relocation step is needed after that) are mainly present on PC. The influence by Long-Range Movement is ignorable after you are really familiar with APPs, but **Dragging** (almost not able to tap, and the fluency of pattern recognition is interrupted) causes more pause. So the general principle of APPs minesweeper operation is, **Make Full Use of Advantages of APPs, Get Rid of Disadvantages of APPs, Cut down the Pause in the Game.** Introduce in details is below.

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)扫雷过程中减少甚至消除反应时间的影响，靠的是**反向点击、双（多）线操作等**，这些概念在近年已逐渐为进阶玩家所熟知（笔者早年的NF教程及张少武的知乎专栏中都有相应论述）。笔者在此尝试对这类操作进行一次更全面的概括：

- **能为下一步判雷提供新信息的先点**；
- **能破空或大概率破空的先点**（其实这句包含在前一句里了，因为破空提供了大量新信息，但为了强调破空所以单拿出来）；
- **无法为后面判雷提供信息的**（即周围雷已经全部判出的）**后点**；
- **需要等翻开某格后才能判断的后点**；
- **无需翻开新的格子即可判断的先点**。

在这五条原则的指导下，优先破空、反向点击、双（多）线操作的原理都不难理解了，第三部分会给出一些例子。由于触屏操作较为灵活（即第一部分所说的第2条优势），上述先后次序在手机扫雷中实现远比使用鼠标轻松。这就是手机扫雷**最重要的“扬长”**，也是最需要花时间和心思去练习的。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)To get rid of or even eliminate the influence of reaction duration, the concepts of **Reverse Clicking and Multi-Thread Operations** are gradually mastered by advanced players, which have been well-discussed in my early years NF tutorial and Shao-Wu Zhang's zhihu column. Here, I'm trying to summarize all these kinds of techniques as 5 guidelines: 

- a. **First to click the cells which are able to provide new information for next pattern recognition**;
- b. **First to click the cells which are most likely to break opening**(In fact it's contained in a. because opening breaking provides a great deal of information, but I solely write it to emphasize opening breaking);
- c. **Last to click the cells which are not able to provide any new information for next pattern recognition**,(i.e. all pattern around it is known);
- d. **Last to click the cells which are not able to judge until certain cell is open**;
- e. **First to click the cells which are able to judge without any new information**.

Under the guide, the principle of Opening Breaking First, Reverse Clicking and Multi-Thread Operations are easy to understand, I will read some examples in the next part. Because of the operation of smart phone APPs is more flexibly(i.e. the 2nd advantage I mentioned in the first part), the principles about order upon are far easier to use than on mouse. **Making Full Use of Advantages** is all the important in APPs minesweeping, and the most time and effort needed to practice.

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)第一部分列出的其余的优/劣势也应考虑如何利用/避免。上文说到**多指开局**可获得极大的增益，这其实不仅体现在开局可以无脑解决多个3BV，更体现在**开局后可以提供大量可判局部，此时可以大量运用上一段所述的点击技巧**，可谓锦上添花。而对于滑屏操作，目前多数玩家的做法是**尽可能减少滑屏次数**，从而缩短停顿时间；但除此之外还有一种有效的思路：**在需要停顿判雷时滑屏**，以此将滑屏停顿与反应时间合二为一。至于手遮挡视线的问题，笔者的做法是视线不与桌面（手机平面）垂直，从稍左侧看屏幕，会获得一定的改善。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)The goods/bads remaining in first part should also be considering how to make use of/get rid of. As I have mentioned, **Multi-Finger Start** will benefit you a lot, which is not only embodied in solving several 3BV brainlessly at start, but also **Showing Many Solvable Patterns After Start, While You Can Apply Many Tapping Tactics** as mentioned in the former paragraph, making your start better. As for dragging, the most common policy now is to **Reduce Dragging As Many As Possible** for shorter pause, but another policy will also work: **Drag Only When Recognize Patterns and Make Decisions**, combining Dragging Duration and Reaction Duration as one. As for the blocking view problem, my choice is to abandon vertical view on desk (or phone screen), to watch screen from the left of center, that will benefit a little.

![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/china16.png)最后单独用一段谈一谈手机全标的**试探双击**操作。众所周知，适当使用试探双击可以无脑翻开大量格子，而这是FL对NF的最大优势之一：若将无需停顿及减速的连续操作视为一组，则FL一组操作可以完成十几、二十个3BV，而对绝大多数玩家来说NF一组操作充其量只是一个三连击。电脑扫雷玩家**有的较多使用试探双击**（例如周丹、Kamil），有的**较少使用试探双击而采用双线操作抵消反应时间**（例如朱耀宇、鞠泽恩），二者均能取得可观的成绩。但手机扫雷与电脑扫雷在操作方式上的区别，导致试探双击的使用也存在区别。在手机上同一个方向连续双击3下（1次正常双击+2次试探）时，若第1下试探未能成功，则第2下试探会变为标雷操作，而在电脑上不会出现这种问题。因此**尽量不要在手机上连续试探双击2次**。另外，考虑到手机扫雷的操作灵活性，试探双击的收益事实上比电脑上低（高级由于雷较为密集，收益变得更低），因此笔者认为当存在其他标雷双击位置时，不推荐进行试探双击（尤其是扫高级时），**在其他位置标双后再回来补双击**可能是更优的操作方式；若已经不存在其他的标雷双击位置，可**使用一次试探双击抵消反应时间**（还**可以标“有双击潜力但尚不能双击”的雷抵消反应时间**）。而扫初中级时若激进地追求好成绩，可以较多使用试探双击，当然势必导致平均成绩变差且方差增大。<br>
![](https://github.com/putianyi889/Minesweeper-makes-me-happy/blob/main/wiki/images/icons/uk16.png)At last, use a sole paragraph devoted to the **Tentative Chording** in All Flagging style of APPs. It's well known that wise use of Tentative Chording will solve many cells brainlessly, which is one of the greatest advantage of FL than NF. If you treat consecutive operations without any pause or speed down as a bunch of operations, then a bunch of FL operations will solve ten to twenty level 3BV, while a bunch of NF operations is no more than a 3-combo for the majority of players. PC players such as Dan Zhou and Kamil **mainly use Tentative Chording**, and PC players such as Yao-Yu Zhu and Ze-En Ju **rarely use Tentative Chording but mainly use Multi-Thread Operation to eliminate Reaction Duration**, both styles would achieve great score. However, the operation difference between APPs and PC causes the difference use of Tentative Chording between APPs and PC. Continuously chording 3 times on APPs(1 normal chording + 2 tentative chordings), when the 1st tentative chording failed, the 2nd tentative chording would work as a flagging(this problem didn't play on PC), so **you've better not use Tentative Chording continuously**(but I will show you a magic case at the last of this tutorial). In addition, given the flexible of operation on APPs, the profit of Tentative Chording is lower than on PC(it's even lower on expert level for the denser mines and less 1s). As far as I'm concerned, when you find another cell worth flagging+chording, Tentative Chording is not recommended especially on expert level, **Flagging+Chording at Other Cell and then Being Back and Adding A Chording** might be a better bunch of operations. If there's no cell worth flagging+chording instantly, you might **Use A Tentative Chording to Eliminate Reaction Duration**(or **Put A Flag on The Mine Which Has The Potential Helping You Chording Other Cells to Eliminate Reaction Duration**). On beginner/intermediate level, Tentative Chording is recommended to apply at everywhere for great high-score, while you are bearing a worse average score and bigger variance.
